My Basketball Journey

Faith, discipline, and teamwork have shaped my story — both on and off the court.

Journey Overview

I began playing basketball at a young age, developing a love for the game that continues to this day. My journey has been guided by faith and perseverance — from community gyms in Williston to the courts of Trinity Bible College. Every moment, from early mornings to late-night practices, has taught me valuable lessons about leadership, commitment, and teamwork.

Accolades & Highlights

  • 🏀 High School MVP – 2022
  • 🏀 Trinity Bible College Starter – 2024
  • 🏀 Team Captain – 2025 Season
  • 🏀 Youth Basketball Mentor & Outreach Volunteer
  • 🏀 Consistent Top Scorer and Team Leader

Basketball Journey Timeline

Early Years

Discovered a passion for basketball in Williston. Learned discipline, teamwork, and the importance of faith in athletics.

High School

Led team to multiple district championships and developed leadership both on and off the court.

College Basketball

Joined Trinity Bible College, balancing faith, academics, and athletics while continuing to grow in skill and spiritual maturity.

Future Goals

Continue using basketball as a ministry tool — to inspire youth and share the message of perseverance and faith.
